About the Education Department

The Education Department of Bannerughatta Biological Park plays a vital role in fostering wildlife awareness, conservation ethics and environmental responsibility among visitors of all age groups.

Through structured education programs, guided zoo, safari and butterfly Park experiences, workshops and special awareness days, the department connects people with wildlife and promotes coexistence.

Our initiatives aim to inspire curiosity, respect for nature and informed conservation action in the community.
